This page describes the terms, conditions, and governance policies for the ICI Collaborative Memory Web App.

Cookie Policy

This site uses cookies for session management and analytics. By using this site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

  • Session cookies are used to maintain your login and chat state.
  • Analytics cookies may be used to improve the service.
  • No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

  • Do not use this service for sensitive or confidential information.

Data Governance

  • For production use, persistent storage and additional privacy controls are required.
  • Contact the administrator for questions about data handling or privacy.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please contact the site administrator.
